“Donors” means supporters who make donations by any means, including online (such as through online fundraising platforms or our website) and offline (such as at events or by submitting paper form pledges).

What information does MFA SEEA collect?

When you access and use an MFA SEEA Site, we may collect the following types of information from you:

  • “Personal Information” is information that can be used to identify, contact, or locate you or that relates to you. Personal Information may include your name, mailing address, email address, telephone number, mobile phone number, and demographic information. Personal Information includes Financial Information and Location Information and may include Technical Data.
  • “Financial Information” is a type of Personal Information that may include credit card, debit card, and bank account information. Financial Information in the form of a credit card number and expiration date or bank account and routing numbers for ACH transactions (electronic checks) is collected only if a User wishes to make a donation.
  • “Location Information” is a type of Personal Information that provides information about your geographic location and can be used to locate the device you use to access MFA SEEA Sites. Location Information may include a zip or postal code, the location of the device you are using to access an MFA SEEA Site (which may be derived from IP-based geolocation), and any other information you or others make available that indicates your location.
  • “Technical Data” is information that we collect about your device and your use of MFA SEEA Sites and may include your IP address or a unique identifier for a mobile device used to access MFA SEEA Sites, web browser, operating system, device model and manufacturer, performance issues, or activity on MFA SEEA Sites.
  • Other information that you provide to us. This may include the content of your communications with us and contacts through social media channels.

Cookies and Pixels

A “cookie” is a small file placed on your device’s hard drive that collects and stores information about your equipment, preferences, and browsing patterns. MFA SEEA uses cookies to remember some details about Users, including but not limited to language preferences. MFA SEEA also relies on information obtained from cookies to perform ongoing analysis and improve MFA SEEA Sites and to tailor our content to your interests. MFA SEEA may use both first-party cookies, as set by us, and third-party cookies, from a domain other than the MFA SEEA Site domain you are visiting. These cookies may allow us to obtain basic information, such as your Location Information, the way that you reached the MFA SEEA Site, and your activity on the MFA SEEA Site. You can set your internet browser settings to stop accepting new cookies, notice when you receive a new cookie, disable existing cookies, or omit images. The opt-out will apply only to the browser you are using when you elect to opt out of cookies. Note that without cookies you may not be able to take full advantage of all features of MFA SEEA Sites or other websites owned or managed by MFA. You also may not receive advertising or other offers from us that are relevant to your interests and needs. Pixels (also referred to as web beacons, clear GIFs, or pixel tags) are transparent images used in tandem with cookies that enable us, our vendors, or other business partners to record a User’s actions. We use these to count and track Users who have visited pages, to perform ongoing analysis, to improve MFA SEEA Sites, and for other statistical purposes.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to help us understand how Users engage with MFA SEEA Sites. Google Analytics uses cookies to track your interactions with MFA SEEA Sites and then collects that information and reports it to us, without individually identifying Users. This information helps us improve MFA SEEA Sites.

How does MFA SEEA use my information?

We may collect, use, or disclose your information for the following purposes:

  • Communication with you: We may contact you to offer information and updates about MFA SEEA, MFA, MFA SEEA Sites, and our campaigns and programs; send you information and material for events, activities, donation opportunities, and volunteer opportunities relating to important developments in the animal rights movement, as well as plant-based eating tips; or get in touch with you regarding any transactions conducted through an MFA SEEA Site.
  • Text messaging: We may use your mobile phone number to send you text messages about MFA SEEA, MFA, donation opportunities, events, and campaigns.
  • Improving MFA SEEA Sites: We may use your information to help us improve the content and functionality of MFA SEEA Sites and to facilitate and enhance MFA SEEA Site features that require User input, whether this information is submitted directly by a User or collected through technologies such as cookies.
  • List-Building and Targeted Ad Campaigns: We may use your information, including your name, mailing address, email address, and IP address, for the purposes of building lists of individuals to whom we intend to send targeted advertisements, and for sending you targeted advertisements relating to our campaigns and programs, whether this information is submitted directly by you or collected through technologies such as cookies and IP addresses.
  • Improving campaigns and programs through planning, analysis, and other operational processes: By collecting User information, we are able to measure interest in our various campaigns and programs and use this information to better serve you and help make our advocacy efforts more effective. We may also use this information to recognize when a User has participated in a campaign and send you information about campaigns that may be of interest to you.
  • Legal purposes: We will use your information when required by law, regulation, subpoena, court order, warrant, or similar legal process or if necessary to assert our rights or protect our assets.
  • Sharing of information: We may share your name, address, and donation amounts and histories with other charities and organizations (and third-party vendors may do so on our behalf) so that the other charities and organizations can contact you for marketing purposes. You can opt out of sharing your Personal Information at any time by emailing us at dataprivacy@mercyforanimals.org or by checking the do-not-share box that may be contained in certain emails to you.
  • We may share your information internally and with third parties, including but not limited to our affiliated partners, vendors, and contractors. MFA SEEA uses various third parties for certain services, such as to process online donations, capture data, and produce analytics.
  • We will not sell your Personal Information, and we will not share your Personal Information with any third parties except in the circumstances set out elsewhere in this Policy.
  • De-identified information: We may also de-identify your information or aggregate your information with that of other Users. This de-identified information is not Personal Information because it cannot be used to identify you and may be used by us for any lawful purpose.
  • Other purposes: In addition to the foregoing, we may use your information for any other reason that we tell you when we collect it or in an authorization or consent.
