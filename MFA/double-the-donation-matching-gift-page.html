Did you know that you may be able to double, or even triple, your gift through a matching gift program?

You’ve just made a meaningful gift, and now you want to know whether you can have an even greater impact by exploring your company’s matching gift program. We think that’s pretty cool.

Your matched gift will have double the impact for pigs like Alba, named by a Mercy For Animals investigator. Alba spent most of her life behind rusty bars, unable to breathe fresh air, explore, or even care for her babies. That’s no life for a smart, social animal.

Many companies offer matches to inspire philanthropy among their employees—some will even match contributions from retirees and spouses!

How It Works

Keep the receipt.

Many companies will match your gift up to a year after it's made, so keep that receipt for your records!

Submit by mail.

Search for your company using the search field above, and download your company's matching gift verification form using the provided link. Print it, fill it out, and mail it to us: 8033 Sunset Blvd., Ste. 864, Los Angeles, CA 90046.

Submit online.

Search for your company using the search field above. If your company provides a direct link to their intranet, you can log in and submit your request online.

Have additional questions? Please don't hesitate to reach out to MatchingGifts@MercyForAnimals.org.
