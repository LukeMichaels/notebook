Resources to help you share your fundraiser on social media

Sharing on Social Media

Social media is a great way to let your friends and family know about your fundraising campaign. Below we have provided some sample images, Facebook banners, posts, and tweets for you to use for these types of fundraisers:

  • Be a Hero
  • Birthday
  • Marathon and Sports
  • Wedding
  • Be Creative

You’re making the world a better place; don’t forget to tell people about it!

"Be a Hero" Fundraiser

Click on the images below to download for use on Facebook or Twitter. Remember to include the link to your campaign page in your post as well.

Here are some sample Facebook and Twitter posts. Use them as inspiration, or just copy and paste them for your own use!

Facebook

  • "I’m standing up for farmed animals and would love your support. Please make a donation to my campaign and help me end some of the worst animal abuse on the planet."

  • "Farmed animals need our help! I’ve started a campaign to raise money to fight against the worst injustices these animals face and would love your support. Please donate to help me reach my goal."

Twitter

  • "Please donate to my campaign for farmed animals. Every dollar helps!"

  • "Help me protect farmed animals from abuse. Please donate to my campaign for @MercyForAnimals"
